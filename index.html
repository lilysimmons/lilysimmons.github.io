About

Hi! I'm Lily. I am a polymath by heart, a tinkerer by nature, and a seeker by spirit. After completing a master's degree in Philosophy and attaining 200-hour yoga teaching certification, I decided to dip my toes in Web Development.

I work in scholarly publishing as an Acquisitions Assistant for the University of Pennsylvania at Penn Press. I hope to take the technical skills learned during my boot camp experience and apply them to an academic field, such as Digital Humanities.

I am currently enrolled in the Penn LPS Coding Boot Camp, an intensive 24-week full stack development certificate dedicated to designing and building web applications. Skills learned consist of HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, Firebase, Node.js, MySQL, MongoDB, Express, Handlebars.js, & React.

Train Scheduler

This train schedule application incorporates Firebase to host arrival and departure data. The app retrieves and manipulates this information with Moment.js. The website provides up-to-date information about various trains, namely their arrival times and how many minutes remain until they arrive at their station. Features a "remove" button, so users can customize what trains appear on the website.
