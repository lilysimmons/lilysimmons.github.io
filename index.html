About Me

Hello world! I'm an aspiring full stack developer with an academic background in Neuropsychology and a master’s degree in Philosophy. I'm uniquely adept at developing user-centric applications from a robust interest in human consciousness and a penchant for analytical problem-solving.

I work in scholarly publishing as an Acquisitions Assistant at the University of Pennsylvania Press. I hope to take the technical skills learned during my boot camp experience and apply them to an academic field, such as Digital Humanities.

I am currently enrolled in the Penn LPS Coding Boot Camp, an intensive 24-week full stack development certificate dedicated to designing and building web applications. Skills learned consist of HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, jQuery, Git, Github, Bootstrap, Firebase, RESTful API, Command Line, Node.js, MySQL, MongoDB, Express, Handlebars.js, & React.
